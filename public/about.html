About Me

My name is Saul Lloren Nemenzo, and I am a Mathematics teacher by profession. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Secondary Education majoring in Mathematics, a Master’s degree in Science Teaching specializing in Mathematics, a Doctorate in Development Education, and a Ph.D. in Education with a specialization in Research and Evaluation.

I also hold certifications in Web Development, Lean Six Sigma, and Data Science, each of which complements my professional expertise. My certification in Web Development equips me with skills to create interactive and user-friendly digital platforms, enhancing my ability to integrate technology into teaching. Lean Six Sigma certification sharpens my analytical and problem-solving skills, enabling me to improve processes and maximize efficiency in both educational and administrative settings. Additionally, my Data Science certification provides me with tools for data analysis and interpretation, allowing me to make data-driven decisions and contribute to research and evaluation initiatives effectively.

I offer a range of professional services, including:

  1. Selling e-books and online courses tailored to various learning needs.
  2. Providing Mathematics tutorials for students of all levels.
  3. Designing and developing websites to meet personal or business needs.
  4. Assisting with research projects, theses, and dissertations, ensuring high-quality and impactful academic work.
  5. Conducting data analysis to uncover insights and support decision-making.
  6. Optimizing processes to enhance efficiency and productivity in academic and business environments.
